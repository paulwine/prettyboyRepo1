Our Story

We are a family owned, non-emergency medical transportation service. Kevin Papuga, owner and founder, started Papuga Transportation in 2007 after needing medical transportation when temporarily wheelchair bound in 2006. After having a full recovery, Kevin was very grateful for the services provided for him. Moving forward, he understood the necessity of handicapped accessible transportation and wanted to help as many people as he could. Specializing in non-emergency medical transportation, we have 10 wheelchair accessible vans located throughout Southeast Michigan; mainly residing within Oakland County. We provided services for: Doctors’ appointments, dialysis, hospital discharges, facility transfers, airport runs, family events, and much more. We accept most insurance companies; workman’s comp cases, auto/accident cases, etc. and are willing to negotiate any out of pocket financing. Please give us a call or sign up to request a run today!

Our Mission

Our mission is not only to provide safe, reliable and affordable transportation from point A to point B, but it is to care for each individual as if they are our family members.
